Common questions

How does the free trial work? +
You get 14 days completely free — no credit card required. We set up Lexa for your business, train her on your services, and you test her on real calls. If you love it, you subscribe. If not, no charge.
What happens if I exceed my call limit? +
We will contact you before you hit the limit and suggest upgrading your plan. We never cut off your service mid-month. High volume businesses should consider the Enterprise plan.
How long does setup take? +
About 5 minutes. You fill in a short form with your business details, we read your website and build a knowledge base automatically. Lexa is live and answering calls the same day.
Does Lexa work for my type of business? +
Yes. Lexa works for salons, dental clinics, plumbers, fitness studios, veterinary practices, spas, physiotherapists, and any service business that takes bookings over the phone.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel any time from your dashboard. We are confident you will not want to once you see Lexa working for your business.
Will customers know it is an AI? +
Most customers cannot tell. Lexa uses a natural human voice, remembers returning customers by name, and handles conversations naturally. Many of our clients' customers think they spoke with a real receptionist.
